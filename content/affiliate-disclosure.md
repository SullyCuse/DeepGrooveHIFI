*Last updated: May 13, 2026*

## Our Commitment to Transparency

Deep Groove HiFi is reader-supported. When you click certain links on this site and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This is how we keep the site running and the content free.

We believe you deserve to know exactly how this works.

## FTC Disclosure

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255), we disclose that Deep Groove HiFi participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means we earn commissions on qualifying purchases made through links on this site.

## What This Means for You

- **No extra cost.** Affiliate links never increase the price you pay. You pay the same whether you click our link or go directly to the retailer.
- **Our opinions are not for sale.** Affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial content, recommendations, or reviews. We only recommend products we genuinely believe are worth your consideration.
- **We may not always use affiliate links.** Sometimes the best resource for a reader is a site we have no relationship with. We link to it anyway.
