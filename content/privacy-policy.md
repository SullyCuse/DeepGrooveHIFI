# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: May 13, 2026*

Deep Groove HiFi ("we," "us," or "our") operates deepgroovehifi.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our site, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

---

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect personal information directly.** We do not require account registration, and we do not ask for your name, email address, or any other personal details to use this site.

However, certain third-party services we use may collect data automatically when you visit. This includes:

- **Log data** — your browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs, collected automatically by our hosting provider (Netlify)
- **Cookies** — small text files placed on your device by third-party services including Google and advertising partners

---

## Third-Party Services

### Google Analytics
We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site — which pages are read most, where traffic comes from, and how long visitors stay. Google Analytics collects anonymized usage data. You can opt out via the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### Google AdSense
We display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at [Google's Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

### Amazon Associates
We participate in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. When you click an Amazon link on this site, Amazon may place a cookie on your device to track referrals. Amazon's privacy policy applies to any data collected through those links.

### Affiliate Networks
We work with affiliate networks including FlexOffers and direct merchant programs. These services may use cookies to track clicks and purchases originating from our site.

---

## Cookies

Our site uses cookies placed by the third-party services listed above. By continuing to use this site, you consent to the use of these cookies in accordance with each provider's privacy policy.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that doing so may affect the functionality of some features on this site.

---

## Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Our articles and calculators contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.
